Process for Website UI/UX
Design Services

Design Interview

  • Assessing the client's needs & goals.
  • Setting up the strategies for creating web design layout.

Design Presentation

  • Preparing guidelines.
  • Determining & finalizing the graphical theme of website.

Website Content

  • Creating relevant content for product specification.
  • Adding appropriate images to showcase the products.

Website Building & Launch

  • Configuring & setup testing methods to minimize errors.
  • Launching the website in accordance with the objective.

Traffic Reports

  • Providing website traffic reports (visitors, bounce rates).
  • Analyzing most visited website pages.

Payment Gateway

  • Integrating payment gateway selection.
  • Ensuring secure online payment selection.
